Welcome to PresQT¶

PresQT (Preservation Quality Tool) is an open-source tool with RESTful services to improve Preservation and Re-use of Research Data and Software.

Note

Development is underway by the development team in the Center for Research Computer at Notre Dame. This documentation will grow/changed throughout the life of the PresQT project. Readers should expect that pages will often be incomplete and/or move as features are actively being developed and implemented. Please send any feedback to the content presented here to Noel Recla nrecla@nd.edu

More information can be found here https://presqt.crc.nd.edu/
